Neural network training to play Flappy Bird

A simple convolutional neural network is given inputs about the location of oncoming pipes, and learns to press the space bar to jump through the gaps. Inittially 100 brids are created, and through an evolutionary process the matrix for the bird that reaches the furtherst is reproduced 100 times each with slight modifications to the parent matrix. Over time a bird will be created with a matrix that allows it to fly through the gaps without hitting a pipe. The matrix for a bird can be downloaded using the 'download' button, or a saved matrix uploaded using the 'upload' button.
